Dear Future Wife

My eyes are blind to anyone else but you!
Even though i have never seen you,
My heart is always in search of you... 💘
I know in your smile,
I will see a glimpse of Paradise!!!
7 Billion smiles and yours will be my favourite 😉
When i want to smile,
I know exactly what to do!
I will close my eyes,
and i will think of you...
I await the day when
I will ignite your heart with undying Flames of Love 🔥
and make you my Queen in Dunya and Jannah! 💞
♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥

IN SHA ALLAH
